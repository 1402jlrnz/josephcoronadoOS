About Me

Hi, I'm Joseph Coronado.

I am a BSIT student at the Technological University of the Philippines (TUPT) with a passion for systems administration and virtualization. This website serves as a comprehensive resource for hobbyists and students alike, focusing on the step-by-step installation and configuration of various operating systems—from legacy Windows NT environments to modern Windows Server setups.

My Mission

My goal is to simplify the complexities of virtualization using platforms like VirtualBox, providing clear, documented guides that I've developed through my own lab experiments and academic projects.

This OS Project website represents my commitment to practical learning and knowledge sharing.
